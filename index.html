About the Game

Weather Match is a unique and captivating match-3 puzzle game where players control the weather by matching colorful tiles. Travel through scenic landscapes, solve weather-based puzzles, and restore balance to the natural world. Unleash powerful weather effects, meet charming characters, and progress through levels filled with challenges and surprises.

How to Play

  • Match three or more tiles of the same type to clear them and earn points.

  • Complete objectives by creating specific weather patterns or clearing obstacles.

  • Use unique weather power-ups to solve challenging puzzles and reach your goals.

  • Plan your moves carefully to make the most of limited turns.

  • Advance through stunning levels while uncovering an engaging storyline.

  • Participate in special weather-themed events for extra rewards.

Game Features

  • Experience stunning visuals inspired by the beauty of nature and weather phenomena.

  • Play through hundreds of levels with dynamic objectives and increasing difficulty.

  • Unlock powerful weather effects to aid in solving puzzles.

  • Follow a heartwarming story featuring charming characters and vibrant locations.

  • Enjoy special events and daily challenges that keep gameplay fresh and exciting.
